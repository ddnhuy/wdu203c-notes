WDU203c Quick Study Notes

Các câu FALSE (In đậm là từ khóa duy nhất)

  • You need to have a degree in psychology in order to understand UX principles.
  • "User Experience" only describes the experience during product use.
  • Design is only about aesthetics.
  • It is important to be good at drawing to use sketching in UX.
  • Stop sketching once you run out of ideas.
  • Always include a pre-test questionnaire.
  • UX researchers shouldn’t assign severity—leave it to product owner.
  • No need to capture screen recordings if notes are good.
  • Design is primarily for aesthetics.
  • Bill Buxton says all design is compromise.
  • Designers usually solve the exact problem.
  • Formative research uses a single method.
  • Formal methods are always better.
  • You have to be good at drawing to sketch.
  • Personas are purely fictional.
  • Excellent drawing is needed for storyboards.
  • It’s obvious which solution is best.
  • UX only focuses on explicit user input.
  • You get same feedback from low and high fidelity prototypes.
  • Wizard of Oz only for screen-based apps.
  • Affordances don’t impact usability.
  • Users need to understand technical details.
  • Defaults don’t matter since users can change them anytime.
  • New tech makes current UX methods obsolete.
  • Open-ended questions are the easiest to analyze.

Ghi chú nhầm lẫn thường gặp

  • Convenience vs. purposive sampling - chú ý từ khoá như “a site's shopping cart”.
  • Gulf of Execution = unable to find / Gulf of Evaluation = unable to determine.
  • Aesthetic and minimalist design: giúp người dùng dễ tìm thông tin hơn.
  • Lo-fi Prototype: dùng cho ideation, true, cá nhân có thể test, v.v.
  • Affordance = feature; Feedback = change; Constraint = reduce set; Conceptual model = hỗ trợ hiểu.
  • Sampling Frame không phải sampling type.
  • Qualitative data analysis: Quotations, Observed, Inferred; Clustering and Finding.
  • Gặp I, II and III hoặc It is trong 3 ý là chọn hết.
  • Nomaza.com: chọn ý nào đề cập trực tiếp đến website.
  • Closed-ended questions: chú ý forced choice, vague quantifiers.
  • Think-Aloud protocol: gặp là khoanh, hoặc có đề cập đến stakeholders và việc observe.
  • All of the above / All of the others: gặp là khoanh.
  • Sampling error: khi bỏ sót một nhóm người lớn.
