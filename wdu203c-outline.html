Gulf of Execution vs. Evaluation
  • Gulf of Execution: Unable to find (what to do)
  • Gulf of Evaluation: Unable to determine (what happened)
Sampling Types
  • Convenience: Easily accessible participants
  • Purposive: Specifically selected participants
  • Probability: Every member has chance to be selected
  • NOT a type: Sampling Frame
Error Types
  • Measurement error: Questions don't capture concept
  • Nonresponse error: Population refuses to answer
  • Sampling error: Missing big chunk of population
Affordances & Related Concepts
  • Affordance: A feature of an environment
  • Feedback: A change in an environment
  • Constraints: Reducing the set of available options
  • Conceptual models: Help users form effective understanding
Lo-fi vs. Hi-fi Prototypes
  • Lo-fi: Used for ideation, can be tested by single designer
  • Hi-fi: Used for detailed testing, closer to final product
  • FALSE: They provide the same kind of feedback
Qualitative Data Analysis
  • Includes: Quotations, Observed, Inferred
  • Techniques: Clustering, Finding (NOT Running)
  • Process: Understanding, Making, Condensing
